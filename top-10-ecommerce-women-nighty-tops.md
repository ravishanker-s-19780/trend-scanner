# Top 10 E-Commerce Websites in India for Women's Nighty & Tops

A curated list of the leading Indian online shopping platforms where women can purchase nightwear (nighty) and tops. Each entry includes the official website URL and a short description.

---

## 1. Myntra
**Website:** [https://www.myntra.com](https://www.myntra.com)

One of India's largest fashion e-commerce platforms, Myntra hosts hundreds of brands offering a vast selection of women's nightwear, nightgowns, pajama sets, casual tops, blouses, and tunics. Known for frequent sales, easy returns, and brand variety ranging from budget to premium.

---

## 2. Ajio
**Website:** [https://www.ajio.com](https://www.ajio.com)

Reliance's fashion destination featuring curated collections of trendy women's tops and a strong nightwear section. Offers exclusive brands, international labels, and competitive pricing with regular discounts.

---

## 3. Amazon India
**Website:** [https://www.amazon.in](https://www.amazon.in)

A massive marketplace with an extensive women's clothing category covering every budget. Stocks nighties, sleep shirts, nightgowns, casual tops, and ethnic tops from countless sellers, with fast Prime delivery options.

---

## 4. Flipkart
**Website:** [https://www.flipkart.com](https://www.flipkart.com)

India's homegrown e-commerce giant offering a broad women's fashion catalog. Strong on affordable nightwear and everyday tops, with frequent Big Billion Day-style sales and easy COD options.

---

## 5. Nykaa Fashion
**Website:** [https://www.nykaafashion.com](https://www.nykaafashion.com)

Nykaa's fashion arm, focused on curated styles for women. Offers premium and designer nightwear, lounge tops, kurtis, and contemporary blouses with editorial-style shopping experience.

---

## 6. Clovia
**Website:** [https://www.clovia.com](https://www.clovia.com)

A specialist Indian intimatewear and sleepwear brand. Highly regarded for its wide range of nighties, nightsuits, lounge tops, and sleep tees in various prints, fabrics, and sizes — often at value prices.

---

## 7. Zivame
**Website:** [https://www.zivame.com](https://www.zivame.com)

A leading lingerie and sleepwear destination in India. Features an extensive nightwear collection (nighties, robes, pajama sets, sleep tops) along with casual loungewear tops, with detailed size guides.

---

## 8. Meesho
**Website:** [https://www.meesho.com](https://www.meesho.com)

A budget-friendly social commerce platform with a huge inventory of women's nighties, kurtis, and casual tops. Popular for low prices, no-cost delivery offers, and a wide range of styles for every region of India.

## 9. Tata CLiQ
**Website:** [https://www.tatacliq.com](https://www.tatacliq.com)

Tata's premium e-commerce platform stocking authentic branded fashion. Carries reputed labels for women's nightwear, lounge tops, and blouses, with a focus on genuine products and reliable service.
