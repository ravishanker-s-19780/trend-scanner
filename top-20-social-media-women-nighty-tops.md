## Quick tips for buying via social media

- Always look for the **blue verified tick** before placing an order through DM or a linked store.
- Prefer brands that redirect you to their **official website or app** for checkout instead of direct UPI transfers.
- Read recent **comments and tagged posts** on a page to spot real customer experiences before ordering.
- Check return and exchange policies — especially important for sleepwear and tops where fit matters.
