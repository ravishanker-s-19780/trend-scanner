🎯 Tamil Nadu Women Personas Review

Nightie E-Commerce Catalog User Feedback

10 Diverse Personas from Tamil Nadu Provide Authentic Reviews

About This Review

This document presents feedback from 10 diverse women personas from Tamil Nadu, India who reviewed a nightie e-commerce design catalog website.

The personas represent different age groups, economic backgrounds, tech comfort levels, and family situations — providing authentic, culturally-grounded feedback on what works and what needs improvement for the Tamil Nadu market.

L

Lakshmi

Age: 21 | College Student, Coimbatore Budget: ₹200–500 Tech Level: High | Language: Tamil + English
✅ What I Liked (4 points)
  • Simple, clean design — No overwhelming clutter, easy to navigate on my phone
  • Quick image previews — Three angles of each nighty help me visualize the design
  • Color circles are helpful — I can see if it comes in my favorite colors without scrolling too much
  • Price shows clearly — ₹500 range is perfect for my budget as a student
❌ What Needs Improvement (4 points)
  • Need more info on fabric — Is it cotton? Polyester? Blend? The description says "striped" or "floral" but doesn't say if it's pure cotton
  • No size chart or fit info — How do I know if this is M/L/XL or free size?
  • Can't see customer reviews — I want to know if real girls found these comfortable before buying
  • No video or zoom feature — 3 angles aren't enough. I can't see the fabric texture or how it drapes
💬 Her Voice: "Design bagundhu nalla irukku, but yenna fabric nu sollave illai — kitta irukku naan pudukkanum fabric info!"
(Design is nice, but they don't say what fabric it is — I need that information!)
P

Priya

Age: 26 | IT Professional, Chennai Budget: ₹800–1500 Tech Level: High | Language: English-comfortable
✅ What I Liked (4 points)
  • Sleek, modern interface — Feels professional and easy to use
  • Filter buttons work intuitively — Like/Dislike/Undecided options let me save my preferences
  • Price transparency — Shows average price + number of products available
  • Mobile-responsive layout — Works equally well on desktop and phone
❌ What Needs Improvement (4 points)
  • No size inclusivity info — Does every design come in M, L, XL? Not mentioned
  • Lack of brand information — Which brand makes this? Amazon brand? A specific vendor?
  • No wishlist or save feature — I'd like to bookmark designs for later
  • Filter options are limited — Can't filter by price range, fabric type, or sleeve length
💬 Her Voice: "Professional-a look poiyirukku site, but size and brand details missing — wholesale-la order panalum confidence kuralai!"
M

Meenakshi

Age: 27 | Newlywed, Trichy Budget: ₹1500–3000 Tech Level: Medium | Language: Tamil-only
✅ What I Liked (4 points)
  • Soft, elegant color options — Peach, cream, and light blue look romantic for newly married
  • Lace and embroidery options visible — Feels special for trousseau shopping

  • Three angles help visualize — Important for traditional, feminine pieces

  • No Tamil description — All descriptions are in English; would be easier in Tamil for family discussions

🎯 Quick Wins (Easy to Implement)

  • Add a "Fabric Type" badge on each card (e.g., "100% Cotton," "Satin," "Blend")
  • Add a size chart link on each design (pop-up modal with measurements in inches/cm)
  • Add "Nursing-Friendly" or "Feeding Zip" label for qualifying designs
  • Add "Tamil" language toggle in header (Google Translate or manual translation)
  • Add "Return within 30 days" and "COD Available" badges prominently
